Luis Fonsi

    Luis Alfonso Rodríguez López-Cepero, better known artistically as Luis Fonsi, is a Puerto Rican-American singer-songwriter, actor and dancer. He is known for his song "Despacito", which became a global phenomenon in 2017.

    enrique iglesias

    Enrique Miguel Iglesias Preysler is a Spanish singer-songwriter and record producer. He is the son of the singer Julio Iglesias and Isabel Preysler.

    ricardo arjona

    Édgar Ricardo Arjona Morales, known as Ricardo Arjona, is a Guatemalan musician, singer-songwriter, arranger, actor and music producer. Their musical style varies from ballads to Latin pop, rock, pop rock, Cuban music, among others. He has sold more than 80 million copies of albums throughout his career.

    David Bisbal

    David Bisbal Ferre is a Spanish singer, songwriter and actor who rose to fame in the early 2000s when he was second finalist in the first edition of the Operación Triunfo talent contest.

    Carlos Baute

    Carlos Roberto Baute Jiménez is a Spanish-Venezuelan singer-songwriter, arranger, actor and presenter. At the age of thirteen he belonged to the boy band Los Chamos and later continued his musical studies in singing, guitar and piano.
